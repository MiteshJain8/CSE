Subset-Sum(n, W)
    Array M[0 . . . n,0... W]
    Initialize M[0, w]= 0 for each w = 0, 1, . . . , W
    For i = 1 to n
        For w = 0 to W
            if w < wi
                OPT(i, w) = OPT(i − 1, w)
            else
                OPT(i, w) = max(OPT(i − 1, w), wi + OPT(i − 1, w − wi)).
        Endfor
    Endfor
    Return M[n, W]

T = O(n×W)
S = O(n×W)
